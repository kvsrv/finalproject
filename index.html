How To Play

  • Click on 'Roll' and play 3 rounds to finish the game.
  • The sum of your dices count as your score.
  • If you roll a 1, your score will count as 0.
  • If you roll a double, your score will be doubled as well.
  • The player with the highest score wins.
  • To start a new game, click on 'New Game'.
